Software Inventory Management System

Welcome to SIMS 1.0, the Software Inventory Management System (SIMS). When locating your technology assets becomes the primary obstacle to utilizing them, it's time to step up to a better solution than rifling though a poorly-maintained software organizational structure that has evolved over time to spread itself across multiple CD binders and shelves. SIMS 1.0 lets you do just that! Backed by a powerful relational database, the days of trying to maintain the organizational structure of your software are gone along with the ensuing headaches.
