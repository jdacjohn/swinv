Admin

Site Administration

Welcome to the home page of the Title Administration portion of the CD Tracker Website. The Title Administration area provides authentication-based access to all administration functions, as well as as Reporting functions. Use the links on the left hand side of this page to access the following areas:

Volume Administration - The Volume Administration area of the site allows you to add, delete and modify information regarding volumes in which software titles are stored.

Title Administration - The Title Administration section provides the ability to maintain all software titles, locations, and licensing information for the software in your inventory.

Category Administration - The Category Administration section allows you to tailor the software categories and subcategories to suit your business needs.

OS Administration - The OS Administration section allows you to specify a wide range of platforms and operating systems supported by the software in your inventory.

Reporting - The Reporting section provides the ability to generate detailed reports concerning your software inventory.
